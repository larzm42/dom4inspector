The six great Daevas are divine demons and emanations of the Destructive Spirit. Each Daeva of the Heptad represents an aspect of their Lord, opposed to the Amesha Spentas of the Lord of Wisdom. Saurva is the Daeva of Oppression. He is opposed to Khshathra Vairya, Spenta of Sky and Metals and principle of righteous power. The oppressive rule of Saurva will increase order at the cost of lives.
